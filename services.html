Services at Community Hospital Lugoba

  • Home
  • Our Services
General Medical services (Out Patients and Inpatients).

General medicine provides acute medical services for adults of all ages across a wide range of specialties.

Accidents and Emergency care

The department provides emergency medical services to patients who may get accidents. It is necessary for trained professionals to handle this with care.

Specialized Clinic (Pediatrics,Gynae,ENT,Optical services, Urology)

This helps our Patients get the best outcome from our treatment and care.

Laboratory services.

In our facility, this is where scientific or technological research, experiments, and measurement are performed.

Free immunization

The immunisations range from birth through to adulthood. All vaccines listed in the NIP Schedule are free.

Family planning services

Both short, long term and permanent methods.We help your clients choose and use the method that suits them best.

HIV/AIDS counseling and Testing.

Participants will receive pretest counseling on general HIV/AIDS information and testing for HIV. After pretest counseling.

Safe Male Circumcision.

Our circumcision is a safe procedure with minimal risks. The treat the most common side effect, that is bleeding or infection.

Antenatal / Maternity.

You receive antenatal care from your GP, midwife or obstetrician. Appointments for antenatal care are scheduled.

EMTCT/ART services .

Participants receive pretest counseling on general HIV/AIDS information and testing for HIV. After pretest counseling.

Post Abortion care.

Emergency treatment for complications of spontaneous or unsafely induced abortion, postabortion family planning counseling, etc.

Dental services.

We provide hi-tech dental treatment to clients, teeth cleaning, whitening, root canal, dental surgery, dental implants, crown, etc.

Surgery.

If you need surgery or other medical procedures, you are likely to attend a clinic or hospital at Community Hospital Lugoba.

Ambulance services.

We provide ambulance with specially trained ICU medical doctors and nurses trained in critical care training,etc.

Radiology / Ultrasound Scan.

We do ultrasound scan services but Sometimes Ultrasound Scan appointment has to be done on appointment.

Physiotherapy.

We have a modern, fully equipped Physiotherapy department that provides a wide range of Physiotherapy classes.

Trauma and Orthopedics.

Our Trauma and orthopaedic surgeons diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions of the musculoskeletal system.

Nutrition Assessment.

We do an in-depth evaluation of data related to an individual's food and nutrient intake, lifestyle, and medical history.

Insurance cover.

We offer affordable insurance services to our clients to cater for unprecedented health emergencies.

About Us

Community Hospital Lugoba Kawempe, is under Community Health Plan Uganda is a non-governmental, Private Not for Profit organization, the hospital offering 24hours services to both outpatients and inpatients including accidents and emergencies.

On average we receive about 500 patients per month with various medical conditions and the majority being children and mothers.
