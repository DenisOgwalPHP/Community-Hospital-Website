About
Community Hospital Lugoba

Community Hospital Lugoba Kawempe, is under Community Health Plan Uganda is a non-governmental, Private Not for Profit organization, the hospital offering 24hours services to both outpatients and inpatients including accidents and emergencies..

On average we receive about 500 patients per month with various medical conditions and the majority being children and mothers..

Vision

The Centre of Excellence for Quality, Holistic Health Care and Medical Education.

Mission

To Provide Quality, Holistic, Patient Centred, Evidenced-based Health Care Services and Medical Education to the Community we serve.

Objectives

1. To provide general medical services to the public at affordable costs
2. To maintain adequate trained health personnel.
3. To collaborate with relevant stakeholders in health training and service delivery.
4. To provide EMTCT, ART, and Reproductive health services.

Our
Economic & Treatment Contribution

Economic

The hospital have patients who live within their community, neighboring communities working within Uganda and beyond.

Staff Development

The hospital support its staff members to study some short professional courses to enhance their performance including Continuous Medical Education (CMEs).

Collaboration With Local Health Institutions

The hospital collaborate with Mulago, Butabika hospitals, Kawempe hospital, Kirudu hospital, Kawala H/CIV, and Mulago specialized hospital for patientâ€™s referral and further management.

Environment

The Hospital acknowledges responsibility for, and a commitment to, the protection of the environment and is determined to make a genuine green icon.

The hospital compounds are full of green trees and grass. Posters are usually used to create awareness among patients about use of electricity, rubbish disposal, water, electrical appliances etc.
